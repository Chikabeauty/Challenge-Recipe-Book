Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Bacon 100g
Eggs 2
Parmesan Cheese 50g
Black Pepper To taste

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry bacon until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and mix with grated Parmesan.
  4. Combine spaghetti with bacon and egg mixture.
  5. Serve with black pepper on top.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Butter 100g
Sugar 150g
Flour 200g
Chocolate Chips 100g

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Mix in flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop dough onto a baking tray.
  5. Bake for 12 minutes.

Vegetable Stir-fry

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Broccoli 200g
Carrots 100g
Soy Sauce 2 tbsp
Garlic 2 cloves

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry garlic.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and mix well.
  5. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
